WHO WE ARE...!

Ratnam schools:

Ratnam High School located in Ratnam High School Pidathapolur, Muthukur Mandal, S.P.S.R. Nellore Dist. is a Co-Educational Middle Class institution affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) on Provisional basis since 1995. The school has been operating officially under the trust/society Ratnam Educational Society. The school is equipped with 48 class rooms and all essential facilities. If you're looking for details in admission/application forms, fees, school timings, vacations/holidays schedule or facilities provided, kindly visit the relevant department of the school.
